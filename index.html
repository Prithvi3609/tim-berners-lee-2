I was born on 8 June 1955,I am also known as TimBL, I am an English computer scientist best known as the inventor of the World Wide Web. I am a Professorial Fellow of Computer Science at the University of Oxford and a professor at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT).I proposed an information management system on 12 March 1989, then implemented the first successful communication between a Hypertext Transfer Protocol (HTTP) client and server via the Internet in mid-November.
